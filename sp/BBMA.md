A relatively large, black-and-white passerine, commonly found in open areas with scattered trees. Very beautiful, but loves to scream as soon as the sun rises.

__Appearance:__ medium sized. Colouration is black and white overall, with iridescent wings and tail. Wings are short and broad, while retrices are ridiculously long, accounting for half of the bird's length.

__Voice:__ serveral vocalizations, including harsh-sounding "yak yak yak," long "wheeeaaah." Sometimes produces complex and quiet series of clicks, grunts, whistles, and squawks.

__Behaviour:__ hops, flees up into nearby canopy if approached.

__Known wherabouts:__ open ground with scattered trees. Highly adaptable, magpies can be found in urban areas as well, such as parks, lawns, and parking lot dumpsters.

__Seen in:__ Edmonton, Calgary
